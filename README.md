## 🥕 CarrotCam — Phone as Wireless Webcam

> Turn your Android phone into a wireless webcam for your PC. No drivers. No cables.

**Features:** MJPEG streaming · Virtual Camera (Zoom/Discord/OBS) · MP4 Recording · Screenshots · Discord Rich Presence · Auto-update

**Stack:** `C++` `Qt 6` `FFmpeg` `DirectShow` `Android NDK`

## 🎮 Discord Plugin Suite

| Plugin | What it does |
|:-------|:-------------|
